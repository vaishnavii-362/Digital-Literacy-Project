##Checklist: Responsible Social Media Use
 
 #Do’s:

-Think before posting anything online

-Use strong privacy settings

-Share positive and respectful content

-Verify information before sharing

-Maintain a professional image

 #Don’ts:

-Don’t share personal or sensitive information

-Don’t engage in online arguments or hate comments

-Don’t post offensive or inappropriate content

-Don’t click on suspicious links

-Don’t spread rumors or fake news
