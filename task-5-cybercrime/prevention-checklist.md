##Stay Safe Online – Checklist (for College Students)##

#Do’s:

-Use strong, unique passwords for all accounts

-Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible

-Never share OTPs or UPI PINs with anyone (even if they claim to be bank officials)

-Always verify UPI payment requests before approving them

-Download apps only from trusted sources like Play Store/App Store

-Check website URLs (look for https) before entering details

-Keep your devices and apps regularly updated

-Be careful while using public Wi-Fi; avoid logging into bank accounts

#Don’ts:

-Don’t click on unknown or suspicious links

-Don’t accept random UPI “collect requests” from strangers

-Don’t share personal details on social media publicly

-Don’t reuse the same password everywhere

#Report Cybercrime:

-National Cyber Crime Portal: cybercrime.gov.in

-Helpline Number: 1930
